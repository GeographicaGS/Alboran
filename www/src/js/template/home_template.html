Mapping of key marine habitats in the Mediterranean and
promoting their conservation through the establishment of
Specially Protected Areas of Mediterranean Importance (SPAMI)

This project aims at establishing cartographic inventory of marine key habitats of conservation interest to extend the Specially Protected Areas of Mediterranean Importance network (the SPAMI network), as required by Barcelona Convention Protocol concerning Specially Protected Areas and Biological Diversity in the Mediterranean (SPA/BD Protocol).
